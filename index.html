01 · ABOUT

Two stacks, one habit: ship it end to end.

I'm a Computer Science undergrad at Parul University (2023–2027) who splits time between two worlds that turn out to rhyme more than you'd expect: backend engineering with Java and Spring Boot, and low-code workflow automation on the ServiceNow platform. Both are ultimately the same job — take a messy real-world process, model it cleanly, and make it run itself.

On the Java side, that means designing REST APIs with a proper Controller–Service–Repository layering, tuning MySQL queries, and reasoning about system design. On the ServiceNow side, it means Flow Designer, IntegrationHub, GlideScript, and translating a municipal department's paperwork into a scoped application with SLAs and RBAC.

Outside of shipping code, I served as Placement Cell Coordinator at Parul University, running recruitment logistics for 300+ students across 20+ companies — the same instinct for turning chaos into a repeatable process, just applied to humans instead of servers.
